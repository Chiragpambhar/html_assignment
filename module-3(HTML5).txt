(1)  What are the new tags added in HTML5?
--->  Some key new tags introduced in HTML5 include <article>, <aside>, <audio>, <canvas>, <details>, <embed>, <figure>, <footer>, 
        <header>, <mark>, <meter>, <nav>, and <video>.


(2)  How to embed audio and video in a webpage?
--->  <audio>
             <source src="file_name" controls>
        </audio>
--->  <video>
            <source src="file_name" controls>
        </video>


(3) Semantic element in HTML5?
--->  In HTML there are some semantic elements that can be used to define different parts of a web page: 
         <article> , <aside> , <details> , <figcaption> , <figure> , <footer> , <header> , <main> , <mark> , <nav> , <section> , <summary> , <time> etc.

(4)  Canvas and SVG tags
--->   SVG is a language for describing 2D graphics in XML, while Canvas draws 2D graphics, on the fly (with JavaScript).
